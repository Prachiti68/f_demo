Product Description:

Track Winder

Do you still use a forklift or an overhead crane to wind the track chain??

Fixomech Track Winder is designed for winding and unwinding of tracks. It is robustly designed and built to wind large and extra-large track chains. It is placed in line with the Roller Conveyor for spreading the track on it either for assembly or disassembly.

Fixomech Track Winder offers simple, safe and fast means to wind and unwind the track chain. The whole operation either starts or ends with the track winder. Whether used with a track press conveyor system, or located separately, the Fixomech track winder is the quicker, more convenient way to finish up track chain assemblies.

You can rest assured that they have been rigorously tested to not only meet, but to exceed worldwide safety standards including the European Conformity (CE) standard.
