Product Description:

Track link Press

Fixomech portable track pin pushers are designed and assembled to make field service and shop repairs as easy as it can get. With help of just the manual guide or a short video you can unbox the Fixomech track pin press and put it to use right away. This portable yet a very “mechanically colossal” machine can unpin the most rugged pins.

A track pin press is a value addition to any track repair workshop providing safety for operators and reduces the lead time to disassemble the track.

You can choose from a wide range of pin pushers from 100-ton capacity up to 500-ton capacity.

You can rest assured that they have been rigorously tested to not only meet, but to exceed worldwide safety standards including the European Conformity (CE) standard.

For more information and pricing please contact info@fixomech.com

Track link Pump

A 700-bar compact power pack (pumping unit) with two-stage flow enables faster task completion. Available options include a manual hand DCV, pendant operation, or remote control. providing enhanced operator safety.

Voltage options include single-phase 230V or 110V, and three-phase 380-440V. Pneumatic (air-operated) pumping units can also be supplied upon request where electrical supply is restricted.

FTLP Product Range Includes:

Tooling

Each unit is supplied with master pin set tooling suitable for tracked applications.

Efficient Operation:

Hydraulic force ensures smooth operation, avoiding damage to tracked applications.

Durability:

The C Frame is constructed from cast steel, with a U cut-out at the front end where the aligning adaptor is fitted.

Portability:

All our presses come with good quality storage boxes and all tooling are laid out in separate compartments inside each box

Double-Acting Cylinders:

The double acting hydraulic cylinder is made in alloy steel and has long stroke with which the master pin can be pushed or installed in one stroke. Industry standard high flow couplings included.

Dual Control Pumps:

All our presses have the option to purchase a dual control pump, where you have both electric and manual handling in ONE unit

Pump Options:

Fixomech can offer an extensive range of pump options, from manual, solenoid , electric, pneumatic (air pump), petrol (diesel) pump and many other options.
