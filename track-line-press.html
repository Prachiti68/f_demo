Product Description:

Trackline Press

The Fixomech's FTLP range of portable track link presses are designed for tracked machines such as tractors, loaders, shovels, and excavators. They are suitable for use with modern machines, including JCB, Caterpillar, Komatsu, and Poclain. These presses are engineered to simplify field service or shop repairs, allowing equipment to be quickly brought back into service.

You can rest assured that they have been rigorously tested to not only meet but exceed worldwide safety standards, including the European Conformity (CE) standard. Buying directly from the manufacturer ensures fast response times for service and maintenance parts, as well as excellent customer support.

FTLP Product Range Includes:

Tooling

 Each unit is supplied with master pin set tooling suitable for tracked applications.

Efficient Operation:

 Hydraulic force ensures smooth operation, avoiding damage to tracked applications.

Durability:

 The C Frame is constructed from cast steel, with a U cut-out at the front end where the aligning adaptor is fitted.

Portability:

 All presses come with quality storage boxes, with tooling laid out in separate compartments.

Double-Acting Cylinders:

 The alloy steel hydraulic cylinder has a long stroke, allowing the master pin to be pushed or installed in one stroke.

Dule Control Pumps:

 Optional dual control pump (electric and manual) in one unit.

Pump Options:

  Available in manual, solenoid, electric, pneumatic, petrol (diesel), and more options.
